Removing News Feed Eradicator

To remove news feed eradicator, you need to uninstall the extension.

  1. Click the Chrome menu icon on the browser toolbar
  2. Click Tools, then Extensions
  3. Uncheck the box beside News Feed Eradicator, or click the trash icon
  4. Reload Facebook
